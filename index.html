Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We've Got Answers

Everything you need to know about AI DAO Co-Pilot

What types of DAOs can use AI Co-Pilot?

AI DAO Co-Pilot is designed to work with all types of decentralized organizations including DeFi protocols, NFT communities, investment DAOs, social DAOs, gaming guilds, and enterprise DAOs. Our platform integrates with popular governance frameworks like Snapshot, Tally, Aragon, and Colony, making it compatible with most existing DAO infrastructures. Whether you're a small community of 50 members or a large protocol with thousands of stakeholders, our system scales to meet your needs.

How accurate are the AI-generated summaries?

Our AI models achieve 95%+ accuracy in proposal summarization, validated through extensive testing with real DAO proposals. We use advanced natural language processing powered by GPT-4 and custom-trained models specific to blockchain governance terminology. Each summary includes confidence scores, key points extraction, risk assessment, and sentiment analysis. For critical proposals, we recommend members review the full text alongside our AI summary for complete context.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely. We built AI DAO Co-Pilot with privacy and security as core principles. We use wallet-based authentication with zero-knowledge architecture, meaning we never store your private keys or sensitive wallet information. All data processing happens through encrypted channels, and we employ industry-standard security practices including regular audits, penetration testing, and compliance with Web3 security standards. Your governance data remains under your control at all times.

What are the pricing and subscription options?

We offer flexible pricing tiers to accommodate DAOs of all sizes. Our Free tier includes basic proposal summaries and reminders for communities up to 100 members. Pro tier ($99/month) adds advanced analytics, custom AI training, and unlimited members. Enterprise tier (custom pricing) includes white-label options, dedicated support, API access, and SLA guarantees. All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

Can I integrate AI Co-Pilot with our existing tools?

Yes! We provide extensive integration capabilities with popular DAO tools and communication platforms. Our platform connects seamlessly with Discord, Telegram, Slack, Notion, and major governance platforms. We offer webhooks, REST API endpoints, and SDK libraries for custom integrations. Our documentation includes detailed guides and code examples to help your team integrate AI Co-Pilot into your existing workflows within minutes.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple and takes less than 5 minutes. Click "Connect Wallet" to authenticate with your Web3 wallet, select your DAO from our list of integrated platforms (or provide your DAO's governance contract address), configure your notification preferences, and you're ready to go! Our onboarding wizard guides you through each step, and our support team is available 24/7 to help with any questions.
